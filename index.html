Car Rental 租车

Rent a car from FTN at affordable rates. 从FTN以实惠价格租车。Pick up and return at the same location or choose a different return location for one-way rentals. 可在同一地点取还车，或选择异地还车（单程租车）。We have branches at major airports and in city centers. 我们在主要机场及市中心设有门店。

Book your rental in advance or contact your nearest FTN branch for availability. 请提前预订或联系最近的FTN门店查询可租车辆。Minimum age to rent is 21. 最低租车年龄为21岁。Valid driver's license and credit card required. 须持有效驾照及信用卡。

Pickup & Return 取车与还车

Choose your pickup date and return date when you reserve. 预订时请选择取车与还车日期。You can return your vehicle to the same branch or to a different FTN location — additional fees may apply for one-way rentals. 可在同一门店还车或异地还车，异地还车可能产生额外费用。

Locate a branch 查找门店： FTN has locations at major airports and in cities. FTN在主要机场及城市设有门店。Contact us or visit your nearest branch for current hours and availability. 请致电或亲临最近门店查询营业时间与可租车辆。

Reserve a Car 预订车辆

To reserve a car, contact your nearest FTN branch by phone or visit in person. 预订车辆请致电或亲临最近的FTN门店。Provide your pickup and return dates, location, and vehicle preference. 请提供取还车日期、取车地点及车型偏好。
