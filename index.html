Car Rental

Rent a car from FTN at affordable rates. Pick up and return at the same location or choose a different return location for one-way rentals. We have branches at major airports and in city centers.

Book your rental in advance or contact your nearest FTN branch for availability. Minimum age to rent is 21. Valid driver's license and credit card required.

Pickup & Return

Choose your pickup date and return date when you reserve. You can return your vehicle to the same branch or to a different FTN location — additional fees may apply for one-way rentals.

Locate a branch: FTN has locations at major airports and in cities. Contact us or visit your nearest branch for current hours and availability.

Reserve a Car

To reserve a car, contact your nearest FTN branch by phone or visit in person. Provide your pickup and return dates, location, and vehicle preference.
